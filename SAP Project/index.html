Welcome to the San Agustin Parish Youth Ministry in the City of San Fernando, Pampanga. Our mission is to help our young parishioners meet their spiritual goals and grow closer to God.

If you love exciting adventures, high-energy gatherings, and being part of a vibrant group that knows how to celebrate life and faith together, this is the place for you. Joining our ministry means unlocking access to awesome youth fellowships, packed sports leagues, creative music and arts programs, and impactful community projects that make a real difference while keeping things fun.

We provide FREE access to our upcoming fellowship sessions to experience the benefits of our supportive youth community. This introductory gathering gives you complete access to our youth activities, team-building workshops, and faith sharing circles. Contact us today to be one of ours not brothers by blood, but brothers by heart!
